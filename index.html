Our Position

We work for landowners — not developers.

Every major wind developer has a team fighting for their interests. Until now, no commercial firm has fought for yours. Meridian Wind Group represents landowners, rural communities, and cooperative members — ensuring you understand your full wind resource value before you sign a single document. We then connect qualified landowners to the right developers, earning referral fees that cost you nothing.

How It Works

From assessment
to ownership.

01

Free Wind Assessment

We run your land coordinates through federal NREL wind data and deliver a plain-English preliminary assessment within 48 hours. Zero cost. Zero obligation.

02

Full Feasibility Report
